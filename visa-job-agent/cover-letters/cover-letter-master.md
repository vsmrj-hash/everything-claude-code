## TAILORING GUIDE

### Research to do BEFORE writing:
1. Read the job description line by line — pick 2-3 phrases to mirror
2. Find one specific thing about the company (recent news, product feature, team post)
3. Identify the hiring manager's name (LinkedIn, company website, job posting)
4. Check if company has any international employees (LinkedIn employee search)

### Phrases that work:
- "I'd be contributing from week one"
- "Not theoretical — I've shipped it"
- "I'm upfront about needing sponsorship, and equally upfront that I'll make it worth it"
- "I'm seeking a role, not just a job"

### Phrases to NEVER use:
- "I am writing to apply for..."
- "I believe I would be a great fit..."
- "Please find attached my resume..."
- "I look forward to hearing from you..."
- "To whom it may concern"
- "Passionate about" (overused)
- "Leverage my skills to..."
