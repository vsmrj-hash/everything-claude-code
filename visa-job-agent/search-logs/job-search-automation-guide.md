## PLATFORM-BY-PLATFORM SEARCH INSTRUCTIONS

### 1. LinkedIn Jobs (Primary — check daily)

**Setup saved searches:**
```
Search 1: "AI generalist" "visa sponsorship" [All locations in target countries]
Search 2: "social media automation" relocation [Canada OR Australia OR Germany]
Search 3: "content strategy" "generative AI" sponsor [All of Europe]
Search 4: "marketing automation manager" "work permit" [Ireland OR Netherlands]
Search 5: "AI content" "international" relocation [New Zealand OR Australia]
Search 6: "prompt engineer" NOT "security clearance" [Canada OR Australia]
Search 7: "digital growth" "relocation package" [Germany OR Netherlands OR Sweden]
Search 8: "project manager" "AI" "sponsorship" [Ireland OR UK]
```

**LinkedIn Easy Apply rules:**
- Only Easy Apply when match score >75
- Never Easy Apply without customizing at least the headline and summary
- Always upload tailored resume (not generic)

**LinkedIn Premium tips:**
- Use InMail credits for direct hiring manager outreach (Tier 1 targets)
- "Who viewed your profile" — follow up with anyone from target companies
- Job Seeker Premium: "Top Applicant" badge helps with ATS ranking

### 2. Relocate.me (visa-sponsored jobs ONLY)

URL: relocate.me/jobs

**Filters to use:**
- Location: Canada, Australia, Germany, Netherlands, Ireland, Sweden, NZ
- Category: Marketing, Project Management, Operations, Content
- Company provides: Visa sponsorship ✅

**Best roles to target on Relocate.me:**
- Companies already using the platform are self-selected for international hiring
- Startups and mid-market tech companies dominate here
- Apply directly AND cold email — most companies list a contact

---

### 3. Wellfound / AngelList Talent (Startups)

URL: wellfound.com

**Setup:**
- Profile: Mark "Open to work", "Open to relocation", enable "Visa sponsorship needed"
- The platform shows if company sponsors

**Best search filters:**
- Role: Marketing, Operations, Content, Growth
- Stage: Series A, B (post-product market fit, can afford sponsorship)
- Market: AI/ML, SaaS, MarTech
- Remote: Yes
- Visa: Sponsor ✅

**Company types that work well here:**
- AI-native startups
- Remote-first companies
- Series A-B companies with international co-founders

---

### 4. Otta.com (European tech — highly curated)

URL: otta.com

**Why use it:** Otta only shows active, real job postings. No spam. Strong UK/EU coverage.

**Filters:**
- Countries: Ireland, Germany, Netherlands, Sweden, Switzerland
- Functions: Marketing, Operations, Content, Growth
- Company type: Scale-up, startup

### 6. Government / Official Portals

**Canada — Job Bank:**
URL: jobbank.gc.ca
Filter: National Occupational Classification (NOC)
- NOC 10022: Advertising, marketing and public relations managers
- NOC 21234: Data scientists (if applying for AI analyst roles)

**Australia — Seek.com.au:**
URL: seek.com.au
Search: "marketing manager" "visa sponsorship" OR "relocation"
Note: Filter by "International applicants welcome"

**EU — EURES:**
URL: eures.ec.europa.eu
For: Germany, Netherlands, Sweden, Ireland, Belgium, Luxembourg

**Germany — Make it in Germany:**
URL: make-it-in-germany.com/en/jobs
Official German government portal for international skilled workers

**Netherlands — IND (Immigration):**
URL: ind.nl/en/work
Highly Skilled Migrant program — employers must be IND-recognized

## RED FLAGS — SKIP THESE IMMEDIATELY

- "Must be authorized to work in [country] without sponsorship"
- "Only local candidates"
- "Immediate start, no relocation"
- "US/UK/EU citizens only"
- "Security clearance required"
- Pay below minimum for visa threshold (e.g., <€43,800 for Germany)
- Commission-only or contract roles (usually can't sponsor)

---

## GREEN FLAGS — PRIORITIZE THESE

- "We sponsor visas for exceptional candidates"
- "Open to international candidates"
- "Relocation package available"
- "We're a global/diverse/international team"
- Company has employees from India/Asia on LinkedIn
- Company's LinkedIn shows 10+ nationalities in team
