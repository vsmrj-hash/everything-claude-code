## What is FOSSA?

FOSSA is a comprehensive biological wellness platform that brings together ancient medicinal wisdom and modern health science. It's designed to give anyone practical, evidence-backed guidance on optimizing their biology — without expensive clinicians or paywalled research.

**Every feature works directly in the browser. No account. No subscription.**

---

## ✨ Features

### 🏛️ 6 Pillars of Biological Excellence
Deep, actionable guides on the fundamentals that govern human health:

| Pillar | Focus |
|--------|-------|
| 🌅 Circadian Rhythm | Sunlight-based clock reset, SCN alignment |
| 🌙 Sleep | Glymphatic drainage, 7.5–9 hour optimization |
| 🥗 Diet | 30+ plant varieties, microbiome diversity |
| 🏃 Movement | Zone 2 cardio + progressive resistance |
| ☀️ Sunlight | Vitamin D synthesis, serotonin production |
| 💧 Hydration | Intracellular water, electrolyte optimization |

### 📚 Conditions Library (45+ Ailments)
Search-powered database covering conditions from Migraine to Gut Cleanse, each with:
- **Global remedies** (Ayurveda, Traditional Chinese Medicine, Western protocols)
- **Dietary plans** — what to eat and what to eliminate
- **Clinical red flags** — when you must see a doctor

### 🍽️ Meal Intelligence AI
Type any meal (e.g., *"Biryani, Chicken Fry, Salad"*) and receive:
- Macronutrient and glycemic impact analysis
- Pros and cons (inflammation, insulin response)
- 3 actionable alternative recommendations

### 🏃 7-Day Workout Protocol Generator
Select your parameters and get a full structured weekly plan:
- **Energy level:** High (Strength) · Medium (Fat Loss) · Low (Mobility)
- **Location:** Gym / Fully Equipped · Home / Minimal Gear

### 📊 Vital Tracker
Log daily: water intake · sleep hours · activity minutes · meals
Export as a printable clinical PDF.

### 🌍 16-Language Support
Full localization: English · Hindi · Telugu · Tamil · Bengali · Malayalam · Odia · Arabic · German · French · Italian · Russian · Japanese · Chinese · Korean · Vietnamese
