## What is Dichotomerege?

Dichotomerege is an AI-powered resume optimization pipeline. Upload your resume and a job description — the system reads both, identifies the gaps between what you offer and what the role demands, rewrites your resume to close those gaps, and gives you an objective match score.

**The dichotomy it resolves:** the gap between your experience and what the recruiter needs to see.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📷 OCR Resume Parsing
- Upload your resume in any format (PDF, image, document)
- AI extracts and structures your experience, skills, and education
- No manual copy-pasting required

### ✍️ AI-Powered Rewriting
- Resume rewritten to align with the specific job description
- Keyword optimization for ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems)
- Tone and framing adjusted for the target role
- Gap identification — shows what's missing and how to address it

### 📊 Deterministic Scoring
- Objective match percentage based on skills, keywords, and experience alignment
- Know before you apply: are you a strong fit or a stretch candidate?
- Consistent, repeatable scoring logic (not vibes)
