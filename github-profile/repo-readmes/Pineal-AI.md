## What is Pineal AI?

Most interview prep tools give you a list of generic questions. Pineal AI gives you **your** questions — the ones an interviewer will actually ask when they see your specific resume against the specific job you're applying to.

It finds the gaps between your experience and the role's requirements, then turns those gaps into preparation opportunities instead of interview surprises.

## ✨ Features

### 📊 Resume × JD Gap Analysis
Pineal AI reads both documents and identifies exactly where your profile falls short of the role's requirements — so you know what to prepare for, not just what you're good at.

### ❓ Tailored Question Generation
Questions generated are specific to **your** background and **this** role:
- Role-specific technical questions
- Questions about your gaps ("I see you haven't done X, tell me about...")
- Behavioral questions based on your actual past experience

### 🔍 Weakness Identification + Coaching
- Ranked list of your application's vulnerabilities
- Concrete advice on how to address each weakness in the interview
- Honest, not flattering

### 💬 Answer Frameworks
- STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) scaffolding
- Talking points for difficult questions
- How to spin gaps as growth opportunities

## 🚀 Getting Started

```bash
# Clone the repository
git clone https://github.com/vsmrj-hash/Pineal-AI-
cd Pineal-AI-

# Install dependencies
pip install -r requirements.txt

# Run the app
python main.py
```

Then follow the prompts to upload your resume and job description.

## 💡 Use Case

You're applying to a Senior Product Manager role at a fintech company, but your background is in e-commerce. Pineal AI will:
1. Identify that you lack fintech experience — and generate questions like *"How would you approach regulatory compliance in a financial product?"*
2. Flag that your resume doesn't mention stakeholder management — and coach you on how to surface relevant examples
3. Prepare you for both the obvious questions *and* the ones designed to catch you off guard
